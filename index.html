What We Do

Manyika Resources is a leading, independent mining consultancy based in South Africa, serving clients across Africa and worldwide. Our integrated team of accredited experts is led by Principal Consultant Luke Zindi Pr. Eng. ACSM, a registered Professional Engineer and Competent Person with over 30 years of international experience in mining operations, project management, technical services, and consulting.

We specialize in investigating, evaluating, and developing mineral and mining projects across diverse commodities including gold, chrome, platinum, copper, manganese, and coal. Our approach focuses on delivering innovative, sustainable solutions that maximize the potential of mineral assets while embracing responsible environmental practices, including the development of digital mine solutions and mining systems benchmarking.
